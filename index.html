About Me

Seek and ye shall find


Hello, I’m Tanisha Sahu, a fourth-year Computer Science Engineering student at IIT Indore.

I am deeply passionate about machine learning and data science, and I have built a solid foundation through both academic and practical experiences. Over the past year, I completed a summer internship at Oracle, where I gained hands-on experience with Kubernetes and cloud technologies. Additionally, I undertook a research internship at the University of Turku, where I worked on cutting-edge projects in medical image analysis and deep learning.

In addition to my internships, I’ve had the opportunity to contribute to the field through publishing a book chapter with Elsevier, showcasing my research and findings. I am also honored to have been awarded the Generation Google Scholarship and the Grace Hopper Celebration Scholarship, which recognize my achievements and support my ongoing academic and professional journey.

I actively participate in hackathons, where I apply my skills to solve real-world problems and collaborate with other tech enthusiasts. My work spans various areas of machine learning, including developing models and algorithms, and I am particularly interested in exploring new technologies and methodologies.

My goal is to continue blending my programming skills with research to tackle complex challenges and drive innovation!
